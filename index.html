Replace Bmminer with cgminer 4.9.2

By a community project we where working for many hours/days to serve you now the world's first cgminer 4.9.2 for Antminer S9 model.

How to upgrade

  • 1. download https://github.com/Zwilla/bmminer-cgminer492/blob/master/update-script/AntminerS9-Update-Bmminer.tar
  • 2. open your antminer's frontpage
  • 3. click on "System" and the on "upgarde"
  • 4. click not on the line "restore backup" and select the above file
  • 5. click on the button "upload archive" now.
  • 6. get a beer and relax, within the next 25 seconds your upgrade is finished!

how to downgrade

  • 1. during upgrading we where creating a new option at the menun "Upgade", open this page
  • 2. click on the link below (download backup)
  • 3. save it to your computer and follow the steps 3-6 from "How to upgrade"
